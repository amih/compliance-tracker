# ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — ISMS checklist

Certification target: an **Information Security Management System (ISMS)**. Clauses **4–10** are
mandatory requirements; **Annex A** (93 controls in 4 themes — Organizational 37, People 8,
Physical 14, Technological 34) is applied **by risk** and justified in a **Statement of
Applicability (SoA)**. Certification runs on a **3-year cycle**: initial audit (Stage 1 + 2),
**annual surveillance** audits in years 1 & 2, **recertification** in year 3.

Legend: checkbox = one-time/event · `⟳` = recurring (update `last`/`next`). See root `README.md`.

## A. Context, scope & leadership (Clauses 4–5)
- [ ] Define ISMS **scope & boundaries** (products, teams, locations, cloud) — _one-time; revisit on major change_
- [ ] Identify interested parties & their security/legal/contractual requirements — _one-time_
- [ ] Publish an **Information Security Policy** approved by top management — _one-time_
- [ ] Assign ISMS **roles & responsibilities** (owner, risk owners, control owners) — _one-time_
- [ ] Set measurable **information-security objectives** — _one-time_
- ⟳ **Management review** of the ISMS (performance, incidents, objectives, improvements) — _at least annual (quarterly recommended)_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**

## B. Risk management (Clause 6 + 8.2–8.3)
- [ ] Define the **risk assessment & risk treatment methodology** (criteria, acceptance) — _one-time_
- [ ] Build the **asset / information inventory** with owners & classification — _one-time; keep current_
- [ ] Produce the **Statement of Applicability (SoA)** — justify each Annex A control in/out — _one-time; update on change_
- [ ] Produce the **Risk Treatment Plan** with owners & deadlines — _one-time; then maintain_
- ⟳ **Re-run the risk assessment** and refresh the SoA/treatment plan — _at least annual, and on significant change_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**

## C. Operations, monitoring & audit (Clauses 7, 9, 10)
- ⟳ **Internal ISMS audit** (all clauses/controls over the cycle) — _at least annual_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- ⟳ Measure & evaluate control **effectiveness / KPIs** — _quarterly_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- ⟳ Track **corrective actions / nonconformities** to closure — _continuous; review quarterly_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- ⟳ **Surveillance audit** (external certification body) — _annual (yrs 1–2)_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- ⟳ **Recertification audit** — _every 3 years_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**

## D. People controls (Annex A 6)
- [ ] Screening/background checks policy for hires (as lawful) — _one-time; apply per hire (event)_
- [ ] Employment terms include security responsibilities; NDAs signed — _event-driven (per hire)_
- ⟳ **Security awareness training** for all staff — _annual + at onboarding_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- [ ] Onboarding & **offboarding** process revokes access, returns assets — _event-driven_
- [ ] Disciplinary process for security violations defined — _one-time_

## E. Access control & identity (Annex A 5.15–5.18, 8.2–8.5)
- [ ] Access-control policy; least privilege & need-to-know; role definitions — _one-time_
- [ ] Enforce **MFA** and a strong authentication/password policy — _one-time; continuous_
- [ ] Privileged access management (separate admin accounts, just-in-time) — _one-time; continuous_
- ⟳ **Review user & privileged access rights** (joiners/movers/leavers reconciliation) — _quarterly_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**

## F. Technological / operations controls (Annex A 8)
- [ ] **Cryptography** policy — encryption in transit (TLS) & at rest; key management — _one-time; continuous_
- [ ] Logging & monitoring: centralized logs, clock sync, tamper protection, alerting — _one-time; continuous_
- ⟳ **Vulnerability management & patching** (scan, triage, remediate by SLA) — _continuous; report monthly_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- ⟳ **Penetration test** of the SaaS (external, and after significant change) — _annual_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- [ ] Malware/endpoint protection, hardening/secure config baselines (CIS) — _one-time; continuous_
- [ ] **Change management** & separation of dev/test/prod — _one-time; event per change_
- [ ] Secure development lifecycle (see `owasp-appsec.md`) — _continuous_
- ⟳ **Backups** taken per policy and **restore-tested** — _backups continuous; restore test quarterly_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- ⟳ **Data retention & secure disposal** enforced/reviewed — _annual_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**

## G. Supplier & cloud (Annex A 5.19–5.23)
- [ ] Supplier security policy; security clauses in contracts/DPAs — _one-time_
- [ ] Maintain a **register of suppliers / sub-processors** with risk ratings — _one-time; keep current_
- ⟳ **Review supplier security** (SOC 2/ISO reports, questionnaires) — _annual_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**

## H. Incident & continuity (Annex A 5.24–5.30)
- [ ] **Incident response plan** with roles, severities, reporting channels — _one-time_
- [ ] Legal/regulatory **breach-notification** obligations mapped (GDPR/PPL/contracts) — _one-time_
- ⟳ **Incident response exercise / tabletop** — _annual_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- [ ] **Business continuity & disaster recovery** plan (RTO/RPO defined) — _one-time_
- ⟳ **BC/DR test** (failover / restore drill) — _annual_ — last: `____` · next: **`____`**
- ⟳ Handle actual security incidents per plan; post-incident review & lessons — _event-driven_

## I. Physical (Annex A 7) — mostly inherited from cloud provider
- [ ] Rely on cloud provider's certifications for datacenter physical security (keep their report) — _one-time; refresh annually via §G_
- [ ] Office/asset physical security & clear-desk/clear-screen for any on-prem — _one-time_

control is in place (or justified out); the operational cadence for the recurring ones lives in
